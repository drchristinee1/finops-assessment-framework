# FinOps Assessment Checklist

## 1. Cost Visibility

- Are all resources tagged?
- Is cost allocated by team, service, or product?
- Are dashboards available and used?

## 2. Governance & Ownership

- Are cost owners defined for each service?
- Are anomalies assigned and tracked?
- Is there accountability for cost increases?

## 3. Usage Efficiency

- Are idle resources regularly identified?
- Are rightsizing opportunities tracked?
- Is utilization monitored?

## 4. Commitment Strategy

- Are Reserved Instances or Savings Plans used?
- Is commitment coverage tracked?
- Are commitments aligned with usage patterns?

## 5. Forecasting & Planning

- Are budgets defined?
- Are forecasts based on usage drivers?
- Are variances analyzed regularly?

## 6. Decision Process

- Are cost insights translated into actions?
- Are decisions tied to financial impact?
- Is there a closed-loop feedback system?
